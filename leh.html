About Leh Ladakh

Leh Ladakh, known for its rugged terrain and awe-inspiring landscapes, is one of the most sought-after destinations for adventure lovers. Located in the northernmost region of India, it offers the chance to explore monasteries, high-altitude passes, and crystal-clear lakes.

Top Attractions in Leh Ladakh

  • Pangong Lake
  • Nubra Valley
  • Thiksey Monastery
  • Khardung La Pass
