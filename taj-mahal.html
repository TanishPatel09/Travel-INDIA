About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, is an iconic monument built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal. This architectural masterpiece, known for its stunning white marble design, intricate carvings, and serene surroundings, attracts millions of visitors annually.
