About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, is known for its regal palaces, beautiful forts, and vibrant culture. With its rich history, colorful bazaars, and stunning architecture, Jaipur is the perfect place for history buffs and culture enthusiasts.

Top Attractions in Jaipur

  • Amber Fort
  • City Palace
  • Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds)
  • Jantar Mantar
