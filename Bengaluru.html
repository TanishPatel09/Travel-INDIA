About Bengaluru

Bengaluru, also known as Bangalore, is the hub of India’s tech industry, offering a perfect blend of modernity and tradition. From IT parks to lush gardens, and from bustling markets to serene lakes, Bengaluru promises something for every traveler.

Top Attractions in Bengaluru

  • Bangalore Palace
  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden
  • Vidhana Soudha
  • Cubbon Park
