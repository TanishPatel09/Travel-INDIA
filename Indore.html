About Indore

Indore, the largest city in Madhya Pradesh, is renowned for its cultural heritage, architectural marvels, and incredible street food. Popular attractions include the Rajwada Palace, Lal Bagh Palace, and the famous Sarafa Bazaar for late-night food lovers.

Top Attractions Nearby

  • Rajwada Palace
  • Khajrana Ganesh Temple
  • Ralamandal Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Sarafa Night Bazaar
