About Assam

Assam, located in northeastern India, is known for its sprawling tea gardens, lush landscapes, and diverse wildlife. From the Kaziranga National Park to the Brahmaputra River, Assam offers an unforgettable experience for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Top Attractions in Assam

  • Kaziranga National Park
  • Majuli Island
  • Tea Gardens of Jorhat
  • Brahmaputra River Cruise
