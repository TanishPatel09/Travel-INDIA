Featured Destinations

Taj Mahal, Agra

Marvel at the beauty of this iconic monument, a symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The Taj Mahal stands as an architectural masterpiece and one of the most famous attractions in the world.

Kerala

Experience the tranquility of the backwaters, lush greenery, and traditional houseboats. Kerala is a haven for nature lovers, with stunning beaches, hill stations, and vibrant cultural festivals.

Leh-Ladakh

Adventure awaits in the rugged mountains, serene monasteries, and breathtaking landscapes. Leh-Ladakh is known for its high-altitude treks, spectacular lakes, and unique Tibetan culture.

Mumbai

Explore the bustling city life, iconic landmarks like Gateway of India, and stunning Marine Drive. Mumbai is the heart of India's film industry and a melting pot of cultures.

Indore

Relish the culinary delights of Sarafa Bazaar and explore the historical Rajwada Palace. Indore offers a blend of history, culture, and food that will leave you wanting more.

Jaipur - The Pink City

Discover the royal heritage at Amer Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal. Known for its colorful streets and majestic palaces, Jaipur is a gem of Rajasthan.

Kolkata

Immerse yourself in art, literature, and history while exploring Victoria Memorial and Howrah Bridge. Kolkata is a city of contrasts, with rich colonial architecture and a thriving cultural scene.

Bengaluru

Experience the charm of gardens, IT hubs, and vibrant nightlife in the Silicon Valley of India. Bengaluru is known for its modern infrastructure, diverse food scene, and lush green parks.

Assam

Explore the lush tea gardens, Kaziranga National Park, and the mighty Brahmaputra River. Assam offers rich cultural experiences, vibrant festivals, and beautiful landscapes.

Diu

Unwind on serene beaches, visit historical forts, and enjoy the laid-back charm of Diu. This coastal town offers a blend of Portuguese heritage and natural beauty.

Varanasi

One of the oldest living cities in the world, Varanasi offers a spiritual journey along the banks of the sacred Ganges River. Visit the ghats, temples, and experience the mesmerizing evening Aarti.

Konkan Coast

Relax on the pristine beaches, explore ancient forts, and enjoy the local seafood. Konkan offers a beautiful coastal escape with lush greenery and a tranquil atmosphere.
